# Marketing & SEO Checklist

- Title tags and meta descriptions present for every page
- Structured data (JSON-LD) for products and pricing
- Sitemap.xml and robots.txt generated during build
- Use canonical URLs and hreflang for multi-region pages
- Structured pricing pages and terms-of-service
- Analytics (GA4 or privacy-respecting alternative) with consent banners
